What is BG Perl Workshop?

The BG Perl Workshop is a gathering of all people which love and appreciate Perl in Bulgaria and in the near countries.
It is the only place where you can come and talk all day for perl. Resolve your problems and share your Perl ideas.

This year we will celebrate the fifth consecutive year of organizing the Bulgarian Perl Workshop. It will take place in Sofia on February 9th, 2013. So come and listen to the magic that Perl is. And also share your mojo with us.

Attendance is free.

And we will have drinks and snaks for everyone registered.

Geography

Our workshop is in Sofia, and that means that people from other places of Bulgaria are very welcome. Foreign guests are desired a lot. If you need a place to stay. Please contact us for more information.

Language

Official language of the workshop is Bulgarian, and we accept talks in English as well.

Talks

Perl events are not only about listening to the speakers. If you are good in Perl, please become the speaker. If you are afraid of a full-length talk, prepare a five-minute lightning talk. Here is some information of what you may speak about: Our wiki page: How to prepare a lightning talk? To submit a talk or lightning talk, fill in the form. Additional information: What are Lightning Talks?, Giving Lightning Talks.

Sponsoring

If you desire to support Perl and its unique community, consider sponsoring the event. Your logotype will appear on the website; more options may be discussed individually.

Venue

This year we are changing the venue and you can find us at Telerik Software Academy

Venue address: Alexander Malinov 31 blvd., floor 2, Mladost 1, Sofia, Bulgaria.
